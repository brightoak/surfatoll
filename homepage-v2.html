The Maldives Surf Trip Specialist

Surfatoll is a maldives surf trip specialist operating in the Maldives islands. Our fleet will allow you to discover this tropical jewel located in the middle of the Indian Ocean. The Maldives consist of approximately 1,190 coral islands grouped in a double chain of 27 atolls, spread over roughly 700 km from north to south, making the country one of the most unique destinations in the world. A Maldives surf trip on a boat, rather than in a hotel is the best choice you can make if you're eager to find the best waves! Surfing perfect waves in the Maldives' turquoise waters is an experience you will never forget.
