Gallery

The Maldives islands are with no doubt one of the most beautiful places in the world for those who like the ocean and tropical destinations. A simple snapshot of the turquoise waters and white sand beaches make it very easy to catch peoples’ attention. Then just add your favorite natural phenomenon to the scenery, and the Maldives definitely become a top priority on your to-do surf trip list. The pictures of this gallery were taken during the many boat trips operated by Surfatoll over the past years in the three distinct regions of the archipelago which are the northern atolls, the central atolls and the southern atolls.

Some professional surfers come often on our boats (just to name a few: Patrick Beven, Romain Cloitre, Alain Riou, Nico Von Rupp, Christophe Allary…) and that is always a good opportunity to bring back good memories. Please note that all pictures have a copyright and therefore cannot be used without our prior authorization.

If you have good pictures of your surf trip in the Maldives, please do not hesitate to send them to us so that we can display them here in the gallery. Finally, do not forget to visit our Facebook page as we regularly post fresh and amazing shoots from the area.
