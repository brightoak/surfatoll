How to contact us

If you have any questions about a Maldives surf trip or Surfatoll in general, we would love to hear from you. For more information about our charters and boats, the breaks you can surf, best season or time of year to plan a trip, what you should bring, or any other questions you might have fill out our contact form, and we will get back to you within 24 hours.

Or you can reach out to us on Facebook or Twitter, just click on one of the icons below to be redirected to our page. Leave us a comment or send us a message! If you have Skype you can chat with us directly by clicking the Skype button below. Note that you can see if we are currently online at the bottom of this page.

Also, don’t forget to check out the videos on our YouTube channel, our new pictures from recent charters on our Instagram page, and our Frequently Asked Questions page where you can find common questions and answers!
