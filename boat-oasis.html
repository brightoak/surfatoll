Trip Period: May – October 2015

The Oasis is one of the most luxurious vessel available in the Maldives. It offers unparalleled service and style for those who want to relax while discovering the best surf spots of the archipelago. The Oasis is ideal to organize a surf trip with your friends or your family but please note that you must be at least 10 people to book it.

Facilities On The Oasis:
  • TV&DVD, stereo
  • Bar facility
  • Reading area
  • Snorkelling gear (scuba diving gear available on request)
  • Open deck with chairs
  • Massage therapist and jacuzzi
  • 11 rooms : (2 deluxe rooms with double bed / 2 standard rooms with double bed / 6 standard rooms with twin beds / 1 with a single bed).

All cabins are air-conditioned with self regulation by remote control. Rooms are fitted with private toilets, shower and lavabo, wardrobe, book shelf, chair and small table. All cabins and De Luxe Rooms are spacious and comfortable. The interior design is light and the materials are hygienic like the wooden parquet.

Safety Equipment On The Oasis:
  • Sonar depth sounder
  • CB Radio, GPS, Mobile Phone
  • Search light
  • Plotting tools kit
  • Compass, Chart
  • Standard safety equipment
  • First aid kit.
Our Price Includes:
  • Airport pick-up
  • 3 meals a day
  • Coffee, tea, drinking water (1 bottle per day)
  • Use of all onboard equipment and facilities including dingy.

We are here for you! If you have any questions regarding our surf camp boat, what is included on the surf trip, or any other questions regarding the maldives surfing experience, please visit our FAQ page or contact us.
