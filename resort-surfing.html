Join our resort surfing from 1st ofMarch onwards!

This is the perfect option if you want to have total flexibility. You choose to come any time and for any number of days (but minimum 6 nights) and you will be transfered to the surf camp according to your flight schedule (international flight must booked to Male international airport (MLE)). The surf camp is located in the North Male surfing area, on the island that hosts the world class spot known as Jailbreak . It can sleep up to 20 surfers. The villa is a spacious salon with TV/VCR and Hi-Fi System and DVD player to relax after surfing.

Resort Surfing Characteristics:

Guesthouse villa located on the Hummafushi Island

Crew: 5, all staff speaks English.

Min-max: 0-20 persons

Facilities offered at Resort Surfing:
  • 4 spacious rooms with ensuite bathroom and balcony
  • Spacieux salon with baby foot
  • Bar (not included in the price)
  • Professional photographer
  • Internet connexion
Packages includes:
  • Accommodation in the villa
  • 3 meals a day
  • 2 surf trips per day in the North Male area
  • Surf guide
